About this mod

Replaces barbaric torture of aquatic life with a scientifically-proven method of fish acquisition.

Skewering live invertebrates on barbed hooks in order to trick unsuspecting marine life into snagging their jowls on those hooks and being forcibly ripped from their natural habitat in a macabre game of tug-of-war is barbaric, primitive, and downright cruel.
